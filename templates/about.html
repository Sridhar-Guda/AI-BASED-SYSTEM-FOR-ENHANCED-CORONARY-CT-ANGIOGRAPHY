About

Plaque classification involves categorizing arterial plaques by composition (calcified, fibrous, or lipid-rich) to assess cardiovascular risk. Stenosis detection identifies narrowed blood vessels due to plaque buildup, crucial for assessing blood flow obstruction and guiding treatment decisions in cardiovascular diseases using imaging techniques like CTA or MRA. These analyses enable clinicians to tailor interventions for optimal patient management and reduce the risk of cardiovascular events.
